about the game

Music Match is a game created by NJ native Sara Abdelbarry. A programmer by day and musician by night (sometimes in reverse order), Sara wanted to exhibit her musical passions in a fun, somewhat challenging game.

You can check out Sara's music here and follow her band and musical endeavors on social media.
